How to Use This Assistant

This interactive tool provides access to historical flood and landslide data for Assam. You can ask questions using text or voice about floods, landslides, rainfall, and affected populations.

Try asking about:

  • Flood severity in specific districts
  • Landslide risks in mountainous areas
  • Monthly rainfall patterns
  • Affected populations in different regions
  • Year-to-year comparisons

Sample queries:

Show severe floods in Barpeta during 2023 Where was the highest rainfall in July? How many people were affected in Dhemaji? Areas with high landslide risk in Dima Hasao Compare floods in Jorhat and Dibrugarh
